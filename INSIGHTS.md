# INSIGHTS

* Tampa was the only city with Gold loyalty customers in the dataset, this suggests that the strongest concentration of high value customer loyalty is in that market.
* The top customer was Jacob Foster who spent $8,722.67. This made him the highest lifetime spender in our database.
* Ethan Gomez ranked second in spending at $8,206.19 total spent, this shows that it is multiple customers that are the reason for repeated revenue.
* The top three customers alone (Jacob Foster, Ethan Gomez, and Sophia Ahmed) generated over $22,000 in combined spending, this shows us that revenue concentration among a small group of buyers is just as important as a company.
* Electronics was the highest performing product category the revenue was at $25,364.23.
* Furniture ranked second at $12,712, shows strong demand but still not even half of electronics.
* Grocery ($405.72) and Stationery ($319.24) were the lowest revenue categories, which may indicate weaker demand, lower prices, or fewer transactions.
* Because Electronics generates the most revenue, it may be the best category for inventory expansion, upselling, and promotional campaigns.
* Multiple employees earned above their department average, including Farrah Garcia, Maya Bennett, Carol Singh, Kira Patel, and Alice Nguyen.
